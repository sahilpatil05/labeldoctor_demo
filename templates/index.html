Food Ingredients & Allergens Facts

Top Allergens

The 8 major food allergens (milk, eggs, peanuts, tree nuts, fish, shellfish, soy, wheat) account for 90% of food allergies. Always check labels carefully.

Hidden Ingredients

Allergens can be hidden under ingredient aliases. For example, "lactose," "whey," and "casein" all come from milk products.

Reading Labels

Check ingredient lists and allergen declarations. Look for "Contains: Milk, Eggs" statements or "May contain" warnings for cross-contamination risks.

Additives & Preservatives

Common additives like sulfites, MSG, and certain food dyes can trigger reactions in sensitive individuals. Learn what to avoid based on your needs.
